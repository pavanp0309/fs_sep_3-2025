Box_model 
how HTML elements are rendered as rectangular boxes on a webpage. Every HTML element is treated as a box, and this box is composed of four distinct layers:

1.content
2.padding : the space around the content 
   padding-right 
   padding-left
   padding-top
   padding-bottom
   padding: shorthand 
3.border : its is thin line around the padding  and below the margin 
   border-top-style
   border-bottom-style
   border-left-style
   border-right-style
   border-style (shorthand)
        border-top-color
        border-bottom-color
        border-left-color
        border-right-color
   border-color
        border-top-width
        border-bottom-width
        border-left-width
        border-right-width
   border-width
   border:width style color 
       
4.Margin :  the space around the Border
   margin-right 
   margin-left
   margin-top
   margin-bottom
   margin: shorthand
